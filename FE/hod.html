Our Engineering Sciences (First Year) Department is established in 2012. The Department of Engineering Sciences provides the common foundational course covering basics of Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Electrical, Civil and Mechanical, which gives every student an Insite on what every branch holds. Department of Engineering Sciences serves as an effective bridge for students graduating from Junior college and poised on the threshold of a career in engineering. We aim towards making students familiar to technical subjects & bridging the gap between school education and professional education.

Our well qualified faculty members of the department have been making their constant efforts to improve the student’s knowledge in fundamental principles and understanding of basic sciences. The focus of the First Year Department is to lay a strong foundation and foster up-to-date knowledge in mathematics, sciences and the rudiments of all branches of Engineering. Besides academics, the department also participates in the development of student’s communication and soft skills to boost them in the competitive environment.

Department keeps Ice breaking sessions in the initial phases of their learning which is conducted by various industrial experts to provide them with vision to tackle the upcoming path of technical education.

The department of Engineering Sciences have proper value of infrastructure and commitment to provide the value education to the students, which is very helpful to develop first year student’s technical skills and eventually become good professionals.
