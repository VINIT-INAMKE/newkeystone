Drone Making and Web 3.0 Workshop

Description:

In collaboration GDIOT, On August 28th 2023, our ACES of Keystone School of Engineering hosted an event aimed at exploring the intersection of drone technology and Web 3.0. The event gathered enthusiasts, experts, and innovators from both fields to discuss the latest developments, share insights, and engage in hands-on activities.

The event provided a unique platform for experts and enthusiasts to explore the synergies between drone technology and Web 3.0. Participants gained valuable insights into the latest advancements, challenges, and opportunities in both fields.
