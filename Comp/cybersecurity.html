Seminar on Cyber Security

Description:

On, Friday 27th Oct 2023 we the students of Keystone School of Engineering had a great opportunity to attend the seminar on Cybersecurity with our honourable guest Mr. Sandeep Kulkarni, Chief Sales Officer at Valency Networks. The program started at 2:00 p.m. with Saraswathi Vandana and lighting of the lamp by the Chief guest. A student from the Third year, Prachi Sutar introduced the guest. Head of Computer Department Prof. Sagar Rajebhosale sir felicitated the Chief guest with a shawl, diary and a plant as a token of respect and appreciation.

Mr. Sandeep Kulkarni gave detailed information on Cyber Security, how the hackers attack the companies and the effects of hacking. He elaborated with examples of ethical hacking. He guided the students on Do's and Don'ts in the field of Cybersecurity. Also shared real-life incidents and many of his own case studies, that he handled to date. Sandeep Kulkarni Sir gave a list of courses available for Cybersecurity. Why it is essential to do the courses to do something extraordinary in the field of hacking was also explained in detail. He informed the students about the eligibility to become a certified hacker, available internships, placements and job opportunities.
