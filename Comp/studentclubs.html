TECHNICAL AND NON-TECHNICAL CLUB

About

Technical and non-technical club activities for engineering students helps provide direction, purpose, and a sense of identity. Objectives of this activities as:

Cultivate a culture of innovation, problem-solving, and continuous learning in cutting-edge technologies.

Provide platforms for students to engage in practical projects, research, and competitions, enhancing their technical skills and creativity.

Organize events, competitions, and initiatives that encourage collaboration, creativity, and innovation beyond the syllabus.

Equip students with essential skills for their future careers through workshops, seminars, and mentoring programs focused on resume building, interview preparation, and career development.


Vision

“Empowering Engineering Excellence Through Innovation and Leadership. To cultivate a dynamic and inclusive community of future leaders, innovators, and changemakers who are equipped with both technical prowess and well-rounded personal development skills.”


Mission

“ Our mission is to foster a dynamic learning environment that nurtures both technical prowess and personal growth among engineering students. Through a blend of hands-on technical projects, interdisciplinary collaboration, and holistic development initiatives. ”
