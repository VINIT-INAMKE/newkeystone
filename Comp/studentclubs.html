AI & ML Club

Guest: Mr.Yogesh Murumkar Bharat, Soft Solution, Pune.

Description:

  • Emphasize the importance of acquiring AI and ML skills for future engineering professionals.
  • Provide resources and learning pathways for students to develop proficiency in AI and ML, including online courses, workshops, and project-based learning opportunities.
  • Encourage students to explore interdisciplinary collaborations and apply AI and ML techniques to solve real-world engineering problem. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are reshaping the landscape of engineering by enabling smarter, more efficient, and innovative solutions. Engineering students who grasp the principles and applications of AI and ML will be well-equipped to tackle the challenges and opportunities of the future.

Web Development Club

Description:

Our Web Development Club is a vibrant community of creative and enthusiastic individuals passionate about the ever-evolving world of web technologies. The Web Development Club is composed of students interested in the Web Programming and Development. Whether you’re a seasoned coder or a complete beginner, our club provides a supportive and engaging environment for you to learn, collaborate, and innovate. The Web Development Club is aimed at current students of the Web Programming and Development program, but membership is free and open to all students.

Objectives:

  • "To nurture leadership and creativity skills in students."
  • "To form a platform for strengthening industry linkages"
  • "Improve and upgrade technical knowledge of students by involving them in various technological fests, competitions, seminars and workshops and thus providing them an exposure to technical world."
  • "To provide a platform where the students can put their innovative efforts and clear their doubts to transform them into reality."

Overall, joining a technical club in college can have a range of positive objectives for students.

No Gas Cooking Club

Description:

Cooking without fire or fireless cooking refers to the process of preparing food without the use of gas or fire. This event focus to create awareness about the nutritional value of food cooked without flame, the necessity and advantages of healthy eating and to encourage everyone to stay away from the junk food. Event focus on the student’s creativity in terms of cooking and encourage them to take part in non-technical fields.

Helios Photography Club

Description:

Photography is a very productive hobby and has proven to be very popular among young and eager minds. Our club focuses on bringing these minds together and providing them with a creative environment to bloom and nurture wherever they are planted. Our main objective of the photography club is to encourage photographic interest within the students and provide them with an artistic atmosphere to exchange ideas and share their productive experiences. Our club encourages anyone to come and practice photography, regardless of their experience level.
