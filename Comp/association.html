Vision

“ To be a dynamic hub of innovation and learning, where passionate computer engineering students excel in both technical expertise and non technical activities. We envision a future where our members, equipped with a holistic education ,drive technical environments, and make a positive impact on society. ”


Mission

“ The Association of Computer Engineering Students is committed to nurturing a diverse community of learners, innovators, and leaders. Through a blend of technical expertise and interpersonal skills, we empower our members to excel in their studies and thrive in the professional world. We organize technical workshops, hackathons, and coding competitions to enhance technical proficiency. Concurrently, we offer platforms for public speaking, teamwork, and community engagement to develop well-rounded individuals. Our mission is to foster an environment where every member can explore, learn, collaborate, and grow, preparing them for successful careers and lifelong contributions to the world. ”
