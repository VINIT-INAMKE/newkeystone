Awareness on First Aid

Description:

The session was organized for the students in order to train them in First Aid, how to give CPR and save lives of people. The demonstration was given by the Training Coordinator Mrs Alka Dalvi from Red Cross Society, Pune. The knowledge on First Aid given by her was totally practical and interactive, wherein students were asked questions after each explanation. The students were also asked to try to demonstrate whatever was explained to gain hands on experience for the same.

People equipped with first aid skills can handle emergencies in an efficient manner.
