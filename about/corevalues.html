Institute Core Values

  • Student Centered Approach:

    “ The soul of any institute is its students. In Keystone, we encourage students for their involvement to develop their individual identity. ”

  • Teacher Guardian System:

    “ We have implemented this unique system where in students day to day challenges are put forth in front of their respective teacher guardian. A continuous communication is maintained between teachers and parents so that we collaboratively work towards future of students. ”

  • Experienced Faculties:

    “ In our institute we have distinctive combination of experienced, young and dynamic faculty. They are well versed with latest technology and are authors of many engineering books. ”

  • Project Based Training:

    “ Innovation in academics always our forte. We all know theory knowledge without application is waste. Brain storming sessions are conducted to encourage out of box engineering projects ”

  • Expert Lectures:

    “ Our college conducts various expert lectures by eminent personalities from various industries and research labs. ”

  • Incubation Center:

    “ As our college is growing and moving forward it provides the platform for our keystonians to fulfill the requirement of companies. ”

  • Co-curricular Activities:

    “ Project exhibition, Seminar, paper presentation plays a very significant role in better understanding of academics. At Keystone these activities are highly encouraged. ”

  • Sports and Cultural Activities:

    “ At Keystone all round development of students is very important. Sports and cultural activities play an exceptional role in developing leadership and team work in students. ”

  • Foreign Languages:

    “ Our college facilitates German, Chinese and Japanese languages. It helps our students to tackle communication difficulties and corporate barriers in global market. ”

  • Industrial Training and Visits:

    “ At keystone it is our proactive endeavor to bridge the gap between industry and institute by giving opportunity of industrial training and industrial visits. ”
