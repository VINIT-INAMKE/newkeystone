About Department

Keeping in view of vision to emerge as a world class knowledge center producing professionals of high academic acumen, committed to professional ethics, integrate and meaningfully contributing to national development.

Our department has well qualified and practically sound faculty, who can share their knowledge with real life examples using modern teaching aids. we have developed fully equipped laboratories ,where students can individually perform practical’s ,test and work on their projects.

To have better knowledge of the subject we insist students should do small projects in his every academic semester. We also motivate students to participate in technical fests.We have developed different kind of laboratories, where old to modern electronic gadgets are disassembled and displayed with information of every block ,so that students can correlate with the theory laboratories are kept open even after the college hours , to keep in view , that it should be utilized for longer time .
