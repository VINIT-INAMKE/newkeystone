C.O.D.E Events

Project Exhibition

Description:

The Project Exhibition was organized by the Electronics and Telecommunication department on, 2023, between 11am to 2 pm in Keystone school of Engineering, Pune. The mission behind conducting this exhibition is to provide a platform for the second-year students, to demonstrate and explain their constructed circuit with model, to the visitors of the stall thereby promoting the learning process with hands-on experience.

The inaugural of the project exhibition was witnessed by our head of the department Prof. R. A. Barapte. Project exhibition was organized by the faculty coordinator, Prof. Suvarna Phule. This exhibition also provided a unique platform to the participating students for their innovations and new experiences with the community.

The students demonstrated the working of their projects using power point presentation to the visitors. HOD of Electronics and Telecommunication and Computer department visited the exhibition and encouraged the students by interacting with the students.

The students from other classes visited the exhibition and interacted with the senior students. This exhibition succeeded in giving an overview of practical applications in various domains with a special attention to some of the problems with social relevance. The event motivated the first- and second-year students to take up the project work seriously and more enthusiastically. Judges guided and suggested oriented solution for student on various Projects

Student Association “CODE” and a Poster Competition

Description:

The combined event of the Inauguration of the Student Committee of the Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Department and the Poster Competition was a memorable and enriching experience for the entire department. This event aimed to foster a sense of community and enthusiasm among students while also promoting creativity and academic excellence through the poster competition.

The inauguration ceremonyand poster competition were executed seamlessly, thankstothe diligent efforts of the organizing committee.

The event began with a warm welcome by Sakshi Raut, the Vice - President of the student committee. Whereas Sakshi Kank, President of the student committee emphasized the importance of student committees in fostering leadership, teamwork, and innovation among students and also briefly shared their vision and goals for the coming year. Prof. Suvarna Phule, Event Coordinator introduced the elected members of the Student Committee. Their commitment to improving student life was applauded.

The students committee logo “CODE” was unveiled at the hands of Prof. Yashodhan Soman (Director KSE) in presence of Prof. Prashant Kasbe (Director KSE), Mr. Ankit Lunawat (Director KSE) , Prof. Balaji Magar (HOD first Year) , Prof. Rajesh Barapte (HOD E&TC) , Prof. Sagar Rajebhosale (HOD Computer) , Prof. A.G.Habib (HOD Mech.), Prof. Suvarna Phule , committee members and department students.

Engineer's Day Celebration with Food, Games, and IoT Lab Inauguration

Description:

The Engineers Day Celebration held by the Committee of Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (CODE) was a grand and multifaceted event that encompassed the spirit of engineering, innovation, and community. This event not only celebrated the legacy of engineers but also featured a technical quiz competition, food stalls, and the inauguration of a MIoT Lab.

The event began with a warm welcome by Sakshi Kank, the President of the CODE. She highlighted the significance of Engineers Day and the role of engineers in shaping the world. Prof. Prashant Kasbe (Director KSE), Prof. Rajesh Barapate (HOD E&TC), Prof. Sagar Rajebhosale (HOD Comp.), Prof. Amrut Habib (HOD Mech.), Mr. Brijesh Kushawa (Alumni 2022), Mr. Yash Bhavsar (Alumni 2023) guest speakers, an accomplished engineer in the field, delivered an insightful lecture on the latest advancements in electronics and telecommunication. This lecture provided students with valuable industry insights.

Following the lecture, a technical quiz competition was conducted, challenging students' knowledge in electronics and telecommunication. Teams of students competed enthusiastically, showcasing their technical acumen. 4 Teams participated in competition named Group 1 - Nikola Tesla, Group 2 - S.Somnath , Group 3 - Satish Dhawan , Group 4 - A.P.J Abdul Kalam. Teams competed fiercely, answering questions on various engineering topics. Winners of the technical quiz competition were announced, and prizes were awarded to the top-performing Groups i.e Group - 4 Stood First & Group - 3 Stood Second. This recognition encouraged students to further their technical knowledge.

A significant highlight of the event was the official inauguration of the MIoT Lab by Prof. Y.R Soman (Director KSE), Prof. Prashant Kasbe (Director KSE), Mr. Ankit Lunawat (Director KSE), Prof. Rajesh Barapate (HOD E&TC). The lab was equipped with cutting-edge technology and resources, providing students with a platform to explore and experiment with the Internet of Things. Demonstrations of IoT projects and applications were conducted, giving attendees a glimpse into the exciting possibilities of IoT technology.

To cater to the diverse tastes of the attendees, a variety of food stalls were set up offering a wide range of delicious cuisines and snacks. This allowed participants to indulge in culinary delights and socialize. Students and attendees relished the diverse range of cuisines available and also enjoyed various games. The Engineer's Day cake cutting ceremony provided a memorable conclusion to the celebration, symbolizing the unity and shared enthusiasm of the engineering community.

The Engineers Day Celebration, combined with the technical quiz competition, food stalls, and MIoT Lab inauguration, was a resounding success. It not only commemorated the contributions of engineers but also encouraged students to expand their knowledge and skills in the field of electronics and telecommunication.

This event was a testament to the department's commitment to promoting engineering excellence, fostering innovation, and nurturing the future engineers of tomorrow.
