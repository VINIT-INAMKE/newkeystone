Mechanical Engineering Department

About Department

Following the vision of Keystone to emerge as a world-class knowledge center producing professionals of high academic acumen, committed to professional ethics, integrating and meaningfully contributing to national development, our Mechanical department has well-qualified, experienced & practically sound faculty, who can share their knowledge with real-life examples using modern teaching aids. we have developed fully equipped laboratories, where students can individually perform practicals, test, and work on their projects.

To have better knowledge of the subject we insist students should do small projects in his every academic semester. We also motivate students to participate in technical events all over India. We have developed different kinds of laboratories, where old to modern machine models are disassembled and displayed with information on every block so that students can correlate with the theory laboratories. These models are kept open even after college hours, to keep in view, that they should be utilized anytime by the students
