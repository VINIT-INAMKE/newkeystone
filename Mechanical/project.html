Project Exhibition-2024

Description:

1. Innovative Projects: Projects like "Design and Development of Solar and Wind Energy" and "Capacitor Lead Forming Machine" garnered significant attention for their innovative approach and potential societal impact.

2. Interactive Demonstrations: Several project groups offered live demonstrations, allowing visitors to witness the functionality and effectiveness of their projects firsthand.

3. Networking Opportunities: The event provided an excellent networking platform for students, faculty, industry professionals, and the chief guests, fostering collaborations and partnerships for future endeavors.

4. Panel Discussions: Expert-led panel discussions were organized on topics like "Future Trends in Mechanical Engineering" and "Challenges and Opportunities in Renewable Energy Systems", providing valuable insights and stimulating intellectual discussions.
